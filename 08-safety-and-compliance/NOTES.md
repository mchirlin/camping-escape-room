# Safety — Notes

## Assessment — All Clear

- **Stream:** Not deep — ankle-level. Safe for fishing step
- **Allergies:** None in the group
- **Supervision:** Other parents around and available
- **Bathroom/Mine:** Dedicated stall with own exterior door. Only group using the facilities
- **Sword:** Foam only — confirmed
- **Shovel:** Real but kid-sized garden shovel. Supervised digging

- [ ] Walk the route for trip hazards, sharp edges, poison ivy

- [ ] Test creeper stability — shouldn't fall on a kid, only fall apart when hit
- [ ] Make sure dig site isn't near roots or rocks that could cause shovel injury
- [ ] Have water/sunscreen available (summer outdoor game)

## No Concerns
- No fog, strobes, or scent effects
- Daytime game — no darkness/visibility issues
- All parents present
- No food allergies to worry about for treats
