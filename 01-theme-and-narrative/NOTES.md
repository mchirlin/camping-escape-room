# Theme & Narrative — Notes

Project: 2026-camping-minecraft
Created: 2026-04-18

## Setting
- Lake Fairfax park campsite
- Real outdoor campsite with rocks, streams, trail loop, trees, bathrooms
- Natural "biomes": trees = forest, rocks = mountains, stream = river/ocean, bathroom = abandoned mine

## Audience & Tone
- Kids ages 8–13, up to 6 kids
- Friends and family, not commercial
- Adventurous, silly, fun — not scary
- ~45 minutes target

## Story
An Ender Dragon egg has been detected somewhere near the campsite. The kids are Minecraft explorers sent to find it. They need to explore biomes, collect blocks, learn recipes, and craft their way to the dig site.

## Core Mechanic
- 3D-printed blocks with RFID tags hidden across the campsite in biome-appropriate spots
- One RFID crafting table as the tech centerpiece (ESP32 powered, battery)
- **Fog-of-war map (1–2 iPads/phones)** — given at the very start. Reveals terrain as kids walk. Game master progressively shows block locations on the map as new recipes are unlocked.
- **MCompass** — crafted near the end. Points toward the buried treasure dig site.
- Recipes are gated — each successful craft reveals the next recipe card
- Game master controls what appears on the map at each stage
- Game master acts as "NPC" to verify tool-gated steps and hand out props

## The Game Flow

### Step 0: The Beginning
- Kids get the fog-of-war map device and an explorer's diary
- Diary describes the Ender Dragon egg sighting and mentions 4 wood planks hidden nearby
- Wood plank locations appear on the map
- Kids find 4 wood planks and bring them to the game master
- Game master gives them the crafting table + Recipe Card #1 (Wooden Pickaxe)

### Step 1: Wooden Pickaxe (3 wood plank + 2 stick)
- Some ingredients already in the crafting room, rest hidden nearby
- Game master shows remaining ingredient locations on the map
- Craft gives them a wooden pickaxe prop
- Unlocks the "Abandoned Mine" (bathroom) — sign on door: "⛏ Abandoned Mine — Wooden Pickaxe Required"
- Inside the mine: iron ingot, string blocks
- Reveals Recipe Card #2 (Fishing Rod)

### Step 2: Fishing Rod (3 stick + 2 string + 1 iron)
- String and iron from the mine, sticks from forest
- Craft gives them a magnetic fishing rod (stick + string + magnet)
- Game master makes gold appear on the map (in the stream)
- Fish gold ingot blocks out of the stream (waterproof container with metal ring/washer, magnet snags it)
- Gold container also has Recipe Card #3 (Gold Sword)

### Step 3: Gold Sword (2 gold + 1 stick)
- Gold from fishing, stick from forest
- Craft gives them a foam gold sword
- Clue sends them to the creeper (stacked cardboard boxes, painted green, pre-scored to fall apart)
- Kids smash the creeper — 5 gunpowder blocks spill out + Recipe Card #4 (TNT)
- Game master plays creeper hiss → explosion sound

### Step 4: TNT (5 gunpowder + 4 sand)
- Gunpowder from the creeper
- Sand blocks scattered during exploration (kids should have been finding these along the way — game master shows sand locations on map from the start or after pickaxe)
- Craft gives them TNT prop
- TNT is used to "blow open" a locked chest (wrapped in chain/tape, "TNT Required" sign)
- Game master plays explosion sound, removes chain
- Inside chest: diamond block, redstone block, 4 iron ingots, Recipe Card #5 (Compass) + Recipe Card #6 (Diamond Shovel)

### Step 5: Compass (4 iron + 1 redstone)
- Iron and redstone from the TNT chest
- Craft gives them the MCompass (real physical Minecraft compass)
- MCompass is pre-configured to point toward the buried treasure dig site
- Kids follow the compass

### Step 6: Diamond Shovel (1 diamond + 2 stick)
- Diamond from the TNT chest, sticks from forest
- Craft gives them a real shovel painted diamond blue/teal
- Go dig where the compass points

## Finale
- Pre-buried waterproof container at the dig site (6–8 inches deep)
- Inside: 3D-printed Ender Dragon egg (painted, keepsake) + treats/prizes for everyone
- Kids dig it up with the diamond shovel

## Adventure Arc
Explore → Mine → Fish → Fight → Blast → Navigate → Dig

## Tools the Kids Carry
- **Fog Map (1–2 devices)** — given at the very start. Game master progressively reveals block locations as new recipes are unlocked. Shows Minecraft-style terrain with fog of war. Real-time GPS tracking.
- **MCompass** — crafted in Step 5. Points toward the buried treasure. Kids follow it to the dig site.

## Stretch Goals
- Magnetic door on crafting table that pops open to dispense the crafted item prop when a recipe is correct
- NFC block scanning for setup mode (tap block to phone → auto-place marker on map)

## Extras
- Minecraft fox stuffed animal hidden somewhere, holding a hint to a hard-to-find block
- Older kids will appreciate the gold sword being famously terrible in Minecraft
