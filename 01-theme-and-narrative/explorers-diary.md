# Explorer's Diary

*Print on tea-stained parchment paper. Handwrite or use a pixel/medieval font. Roll into a scroll or fold into a small leather-bound booklet.*

---

**Explorer's Log — Day 47**

We've been tracking strange energy readings across the forest biomes near Lake Fairfax for weeks now. Enderman sightings. Purple particles in the air at night. Something big is happening.

**Day 51**

Found it. The readings are off the charts. There is an Ender Dragon egg buried somewhere in this area. An actual egg. I don't know how it got here — maybe a portal opened, maybe it's been here for centuries. But it's real, and it's close.

**Day 52**

I've been exploring the biomes around the campsite. There's a forest thick with oak and birch. A stream that runs through the valley. I even found what looks like an old abandoned mine near the north end — boarded up, dark inside. I'm not going in there without a pickaxe.

I've started gathering wood planks from fallen trees in the forest. I've hidden 4 of them nearby in case I need them later. Marked their locations on my map.

**Day 53**

Bad news. My compass broke crossing the stream and I lost most of my tools. I can't dig, I can't mine, I can't fish. I'll need to craft everything from scratch.

Worse news — I spotted a Creeper on the trail this morning. A big one. It hasn't seen me yet, but I'm going to need a sword before I deal with that thing.

Good news — I found an old crafting table in a storage tent. Still works. If I can find the right blocks, I can craft everything I need.

**Day 54 — FINAL ENTRY**

I can't do this alone. I'm leaving this diary, my map, and the crafting table for whoever finds them. The egg is out there. I know it.

Here's what you need to know:

- **The map** will reveal the terrain as you explore. I've marked where I hid the wood planks.
- **Find 4 wood planks** in the forest and bring them to the Villager at camp. He'll give you the crafting table and your first recipe.
- **Craft tools, explore biomes, collect blocks.** Each craft unlocks the next step.
- **Watch out for the Creeper.** You'll need a sword.
- **The egg is buried.** You'll need a compass to find it and a shovel to dig it up.

Good luck, explorers. The Ender Dragon egg is counting on you.

— S.
